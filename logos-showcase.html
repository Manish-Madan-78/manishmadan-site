Design notes

  • Logo 1 works best at full width — nav bars, email signatures, letterheads. The icon scales independently for favicons.
  • Logo 2 works best as a standalone mark — hero headers, social profile images, presentation covers. The MM monogram is distinctive at any size.
  • Amber (#c9933a) holds contrast on both dark and light backgrounds — no variant needed for the accent color.
  • Text switches between #e8e4dc (dark bg) and #0f1117 (light bg). Both SVG files are pre-set for dark backgrounds; swap the fill values for a light-bg export.
  • To use in the website nav: inline the SVG directly into index.html for best rendering and no HTTP request.
